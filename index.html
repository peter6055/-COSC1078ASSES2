IT WORK Interview questions

This interviewee is an IT professional from a private company.

  • What kind of work is done by the IT professional? Can you please tell us about a recent project or process that you made better, faster, smarter or more efficient?
  • What kinds of people does the IT professional interact with? Are they other IT professionals? Clients? Investors?  The general public?
  • Where does the IT professional spend most of their time?
  • What aspect of their position is most challenging?
  • If you’ve just been assigned to a project involving a new technology that you don't know. What steps have you decided to overcome a knowledge gap and ensure that you can finish on time?
  • How do you manage your work-life balance?
  • How do you think further technology advances will impact your job?
  • I heard that most 35-year-old IT employees without new technology will be fired. How do you keep your technology skills current?
  • What technology-related blogs or websites do you follow? What is your favourite and least favourite technology product, and why?
  • What do you think of your current job? Does it feel struggling?
