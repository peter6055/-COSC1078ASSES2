SHOPMY - SHOP N BUY. LET'S SHOPMY.

Melbourne based online shopping platfrom for Asian.

Visit Project Website: https://www.shopmy.com.au


Overview

SHOPMY is a project starting from year 2019. We are a melbourne-based peer to peer shopping platform for everyone to buy and sell their products. We stand in a different position than other common shopping platforms. We refused slow and low quality logistics. We hate a not accelerated disputes processed. We don’t disagree about people “paid” first and “receive” after. We decide to make things differently.


Motivation

According to the official research in 2019, Melbourne's online shopping market is expected to reach $35 billion by 2021, with an average increase of 2.5% per year. Furthermore, 400,000 are Asian (includes Chinese, Southeast Asian) and are growing steadily in the following 7 years. [1] (SHOPMY PTY LTD unpublic whitepaper p.4) As our research of 800 samples, we had found out over 70% of people think that there should be an improvement of online shopping in Melbourne. They complain about high freight, slow logistics and inefficient customer service. [2] (SHOPMY PTY LTD unpublic whitepaper p.5-6). Our customer behavior research, we realized that the “culture” of shopping was different between easten and westen people’s minds. People who live in Asian countries own a different understanding of user experience than western people, such as Taobao in China and Shopee in south asia and Carousell in Singapore. (UI design and process different). For example, Taobao, Ging Dung, Tmall and Xian Yu, they use similar layouts in their UI Design. These layouts are totally different from ebay or Amazon design. Moreover, after we studied user understanding we found process flow also influenced users too. Take Shopee, a shopping platform that is successful in 8 countries (https://shopee.com) as an example. It provides a third party payment platform for users to pay into an official bank account first rather than pay to the seller directly. As a result of our study, current shopping platforms that exist in Melbourne are not able to fully target Asian customers. Over 73% of Asian want to try something different and the user experience they understand.



Description

Logistic System

Firstly, our company provides its own logistics company, we can deal with the items within 20kg and 160cm in 5 dollars and delivered within 48 hrs.


Banking System

Secondly, users don’t need to pay directly into the seller's paypal or bank account. We provide more security payments by adding a trust account between seller and buyer. Every transaction will pass through our 3rd bank account. We will release the money after the buyer received the product without any problem. In this case, we can guarantee both seller’s and buyer’s security of using our platform. Also, as we have our own cashing system, we can provide less surcharge to customers and sellers. Currently, our selling fees are 1/30 less and buying fees is 1/3 less than competitors.


User Experience

Thirdly, we spend a lot of time on doing user experience research and making our app easy for Aisan to accept it. Not just process flow and UI, we bring even more than that. We provide group buying functions that are popular in Asian. Using our system can easily upload and seller group buying products and avoid people didn’t showing up after the group buying (In our case study, there are over 70% of sellers who don't collect deposits before group buying. Even if you collect deposits by bank transfer, the customer may be concerned about the trustability of the seller.). Finally, don’t forget our core values. Everyone can sell, everyone can buy. Using our system can avoid paying a high volume of selling fees. Also, you have more time to focus on your product. You don’t need to worry about anything else.



Tool and Tech.

In our front-end system, we build it with vue.js in mobile clients. Back-end are built by PHP and mangoDB.

Computing Engine

As SHOPMY needs to be able to handle thousands of sessions doing online shopping at the same time. Google cloud platform compute engine supports us for high performance computing. In this case, we had built an extra virtual machine load balancer. As the budgets are limited, build load balancers can provide a high availability working environment and also provide session balance protection (it won’t be like HA one machine is standby but not working). We had also built 2 load balancers in vpc to avoid overload in a single network interface. All the internet traffic is protected by CDN in cloudflare.


Bucket Storage

As we think about there might be images transferred every second, using VM built in SSD and Internet to dealing images is not a reliable solution. Combining images in a VM SSD can cost 20x higher budget than using a bucket. Also, backing up several TB (and it might be growing) can cause difficulty for repair if there are data damage accidents (VM snapshot and backup storage are more expensive than bucket). After that we decided to use AWS S3 technology (10x cheaper than GCS and Azure) for storing our large volume of images and videos.


Future

In future, we will separate HA and load balancers when our VM structure is more complicated. Also try to transfer our app into the App Engine and separate database into CloudSQL for a more specific computing environment.
